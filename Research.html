Research

During my internship at the HIVE Lab in George Washing University, I was able to accomplish things in both Biology and Computer Science. To start, I worked with code that would utilize AI/Machine Learning to look at hundreds of data points regarding bacteria found in microbiomes of the body. My code worked to analyze through all these data points and find specific bacteria that seemed to be significantly present in the gut and liver microbiome in patients with certain illnesses. After collecting these data points through the program, the lab was able to look more indepth into these specific species and see if they held signifigance in the gut/liver through actual lab work (which sadly I could not participate in due to the pandemic). Additionally, utilizing the program I had helped write, our team also worked to design a general application that doctors could start to use, where doctors could enter in patient information, and compare patient data to the wide variety of already existing data points to make a more informed diagnosis on the patient's illness. Utilizing MATLab, I was able to create the back end AI program to monitor data points and created an application that presented a friendly user interface (due to the app still being in development by the team it is not shown) that utilized this AI program. Finally, nearing the end of my internship, we realized the limitations of using MATLAB, and were looking into transfering the script into R. I started to do initial work on this, but was sadly stopped due to the end of my internship.
